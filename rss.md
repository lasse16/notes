# RSS

A [[xml]] sub-format, primarily used for news-feeds.

It is used to spread news and updates across multiple subscribers.

While it is possible to manually subscribe to a feed, [Readers](#Readers) are commonly used to aggregate multiple feeds and check for updates.

## Readers

- [Feedly][feedly]:
This is the reader I currently use.

- [FreshRSS][fresh_rss]:
An open-source, self-hostable offering, which focusses on feed aggregation.
