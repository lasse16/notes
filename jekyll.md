# Jekyll

A [Static-site generator](static-site-generator), written in [Ruby](ruby), transforming [[markdown]] into websites.

It is targeted at blogs and has features, like permalinks, categories and posts built-in.

[Github Pages](github-pages) also uses it to transform [[markdown]] files.

Website templates are written in [Liquid](liquid) and plugins in [Ruby](ruby).
