# Raster graphics

These are a technique to store images.

They store colors per pixel on a predefined pixel grid.

With this underlying way of storage, they are not losslessly scalable unless the new image is an integer multiple of the original size.

Its concept is simpler, if you are applying operations, like blurring, as one they are essentially a giant lookup table for the pixel values.

Common formats are [[jpeg]] and [[png]].
