# Choosing a postgres operator

A comparison of [[kubernetes]] postgres operators, with a focus on automatic updates and scheduled backups.

Comparing [cruncy-operator], [[cloudnativepg-postgresql-operator-for-kubernetes]], and the [zalando-operator], the author chose the [crunchy-operator] because it is the oldest operator available.
