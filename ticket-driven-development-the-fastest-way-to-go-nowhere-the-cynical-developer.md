# Ticket-Driven Development: The Fastest Way to Go Nowhere | The Cynical Developer

A cynical post about tickets and "Dienst nach Vorschrift".

It leads to developers, who do not think outside of refinements and the tickets scope.

There is no refactoring, no improvement, only technical debt until the ticket queue is empty, which it never is.
